Welcome! This training will help you understand how to effectively use Large Language Models (LLMs) in your daily work.

## What You'll Learn

This training covers the essential skills and knowledge for working with ChatGPT and other LLMs:

- **Core concepts** - tokens, context windows, and how LLMs generate responses
- **Managing context** effectively to get better results
- **Using tools and agentic behavior** - Canvas, GPTs, Deep Research, and more
- **Practical prompt patterns** that work in real-world scenarios
- **Real-world integration strategies** for your work
